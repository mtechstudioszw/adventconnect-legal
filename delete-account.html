Delete Your Account & Data

How to permanently remove your account and personal data.

You can delete your Advent Connect ZW account and personal data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Advent Connect ZW and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Open Account and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and personal data are permanently removed.

Option 2 — By email request

If you can't access the app, email us from the address on your account and we'll delete it for you:

Send to tanatswamichaelmikuwa@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my account". We verify the request comes from your account email, then complete the deletion. We aim to action requests within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in credentials.
  • Your profile: name, email, date of birth, photos, bio, and settings.
  • Your personal content tied to the account (your posts, listings, jobs, prayers, stories, and messages), subject to the retention note below.

What may be retained

We may retain limited information where required to comply with the law, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, or enforce our terms — for example, records of a moderation action. Retained data is kept only as long as necessary and is then deleted.

Timeline

PERMANENT  In-app deletion is immediate. Email requests are completed within 7 days. Account deletion cannot be undone.
